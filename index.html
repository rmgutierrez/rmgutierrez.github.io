Academic Projects

Fluenty

Fluenty is a platform designed to enhance English language education in Brazil by connecting native Canadian English instructors with Brazilian students through their schools. Many students struggle with limited real-world interaction with native speakers, which affects their fluency and confidence. Fluenty aims to solve this by offering a web-based platform that facilitates session scheduling, progress tracking, gamified learning, and personalized feedback. The primary users of this platform will be school administrators, instructors, and students. The system will be accessed via a web browser and will integrate Zoom for virtual classes, allowing seamless communication between students and instructors.
